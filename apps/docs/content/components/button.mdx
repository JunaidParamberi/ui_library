## Props

| Prop | Type | Default |
|------|------|---------|
| `variant` | `"primary" \| "secondary" \| "ghost" \| "danger" \| "outline"` | `"secondary"` |
| `size` | `"sm" \| "default" \| "lg" \| "icon" \| "icon-sm" \| "icon-lg"` | `"default"` |
| `loading` | `boolean` | `false` |
| `asChild` | `boolean` | `false` |
