## Props

`Dialog`, `DialogTrigger`, `DialogClose` are Radix `Root`/`Trigger`/`Close` re-exports. `DialogContent` extends Radix `Content` props plus `className`. `DialogHeader` and `DialogFooter` are plain layout `div`s accepting `React.HTMLAttributes<HTMLDivElement>`. `DialogTitle` and `DialogDescription` extend their Radix counterparts plus `className`.
